list = [1, 2, 3, 4, 5]
new_list = []
for num in list:
    if num % 4 == 2:
        new_list.append("new")
print(new_list)

['new']

Over here, we have an iteration through the "for num in list", as it is iterating through the entire list.

The "if num % 4 == 2" is a selection, because it is selecting a specific number.
